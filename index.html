Dash Experience

Completing Dash was equal parts challenging, interesting, informative, and cool. I enjoyed learning more HTML and CSS as I do have some foundation for those languages. Where I had most trouble was with JavaScript. I do enjoy the logical thinking, but sometimes it was a bit tough to grasp the bigger concepts.

Overall, I thought the whole thing was extremely well-made and I would do it again in a heart-beat. Dash got me more excited about the prospects of joining General Assembly for Web Development immersion.
